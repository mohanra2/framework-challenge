Frequently Asked Questions

>> Is parking free?

Parking is free for any guest that has booked a room with Skyline. Parking with valet will be included for the duration of the stay, and additional charges will be made if parking is needed for more time.

>> Is free continental breakfast included with the stay?

Free breakfast is included with every stay. Meal vouchers are distributed with each reservation and foods are clearly labelled with all allergens and ingredients for safe consumption.

>> How often is the pool cleaned?

The pool and surrounding area is santized daily, with weekly deep cleansing and drainings as needed.
